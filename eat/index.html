--- layout: page title: stuff I like/reference a lot --- After reading "The Selfish Gene" in high school, I realized that some models can feel better than others, and that I could go 'shopping' for better models in books for which to better organize and navigate the world -- mental interior design, if you will. These are some of the best places to raid, imo.
